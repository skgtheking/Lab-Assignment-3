Bio

I am Shubham Gupta, a passionate explorer of the world and a dedicated international student at the University of Idaho. From a young age, I have been fascinated by the wonders of science and the beauty of nature. My journey began with a simple camera lens and a curiosity that knew no bounds. As I delved deeper into the Moscow campus, I discovered a profound appreciation for the intricate web of life that surrounds us.

My classes have taken me to the far corners of the University, from the crowded Eatery at the Wallace to the isolated golf course on the other side of the campus.

This website is a reflection of my passion for the campus and my desire to share the wonders of the natural campus with others. Join me on this journey of discovery as we explore the mysteries of Moscow, one fascinating detail at a time.
